// section 01 — the problem
308 teams. 6,700+ games. How do you rank them all?
Simple win% doesn't work — a team that goes 30-5 against weak opponents shouldn't rank above a team that goes 28-10 beating elite competition. But how do you measure "elite"? Every team's quality depends on their opponents' quality, which depends on their opponents' quality. It's circular. The Colley Matrix solves all 308 ratings simultaneously as a system of linear equations.

// section 04 — why this beats rpi
No arbitrary weights
RPI weights strength of schedule at 75% — a team can look great just by playing tough opponents, even with a losing record. VBI lets SOS emerge naturally from the math. Your rating is determined entirely by who you beat and where you beat them. No human input. No committees. Just the matrix.
